What Have I Done For the Company?

Business is all about performance and I strive each day to help the business perform at it's peak levels. As the business analyst, it is my job to analyze business activity and provide both detailed reports and BI Solutions. Below are some of the more impactful accomplishments I've had at the company.

System Implementations

Implementing Software is no easy task. I've taken project lead on both of our major software migrations, from Jonas to Viewpoint and now from Spruceware to Biztrack. I've also been a major player in a majority of our business requirements meetings and pursued new softwares including Microsoft Dynamics, Power BI, Keystyle, and HR4.

Integration of Microsoft Power BI.

Power BI has become the forefront of our data solutions at Macdonald Group of Companies. I've successfully integrated the software and built reports and dashboards for financial statements, forecasting, business operations, and sales and marketing. I've taught other employees how to use the program and consistently developed new and innovative Power BI dashboards.

Financial Server

I built a financial server from scratch that directly connects to our SQL databases. This gives us the ability to generate "real time" financials at the click of a button. Income Statements, Balance Sheets, Cash Flows, Forecasts and Budget are all available on this server and filterable based on fiscal years, months and individual companies.

Restructuring of Corporate Incentives

A year ago I proposed major changes to our corporate incentives plan. The change was made to help align employee goals with the corporate goals. This plan included the introduction of a Group RRSP plan, a corporate wide company hierarchy leveling system with guidelines for bonus payouts and salary ranges and a strategic initiative to align and develop operational goals for each department/team/individual and reward exceptional performance on an annual basis.
