Business Analyst

Description

As the business analyst I'm expected to assist with the implementation and support of business information systems across multiple departments. I'm expected to identify problems and opportunities within the company and provide solutions that help achieve the corporations goals. The job requires a collaborating effort with financial reporting and information technology to develop initiatives and strategies that optimise costs and improve internal and external reporting.

Duties & Responsibilities

Business analysts conduct both internal and external analysis. This includes market analysis, industry analysis, analyzing business results and forecasting business futures. In addition, they develop and monitor data metrics and ensure business data and reporting needs are met.

  • Creating Detailed Business Analysis, outlining problems, opportunities and solutions for the business.
  • Budgeting & Forecasting
  • Planning & Monitoring
  • Financial Modelling
  • Variance Analysis
  • Reporting
  • Gathering Business Requirements and reporting them back to stakeholders
