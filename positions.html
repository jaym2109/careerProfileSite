Business Analyst

Macdonald Group of Companies

Business Integration Analyst

Description

As the business analyst I'm expected to lead the implementation and support of business information systems across all corporations within the Macdonald Group of Companies family. I'm expected to identify problems and opportunities within the company and provide solutions that help achieve the corporations goals. The job requires a collaborating effort with financial reporting and information technology to develop initiatives and strategies that optimise costs and improve internal and external reporting.

Duties & Responsibilities

Business Intelligence & Reporting (30%)
  • Maintain, test and develop a variety of Power BI reports.
  • Develop forms and reports to be utilized within Bistrack and Viewpoint using Crystal Reports.
  • Create and maintain the financial server that hosts our ad-hoc financial statements
  • Maintain the corporations entire eco-system of data across a variety of databases throughout multiple information systems.
  • Develop custom SQL queries to extract valuable information from our systems.
Information System Management (30%)
  • Manage all information systems throughout the corporation.
  • Create, secure and administer all user accounts within the corporation.
  • Develop and maintain standard operating procedural manuals for corporate IS systems.
  • Make any additional customizations to the systems including user defined fields, tables, views, forms, reports and notifiers.
  • Liaison with technical support to fix any end user problems.
Business Systems Analysis (20%)
  • Identify problems and opportunities within the corporations technological infrastructure and bring forth potential solutions.
  • Liaison with management and department members to gather requirements for potential technological changes.
  • Meet with product sales managers while pursuing potential software changes.
  • Perform cost-benefit analysis and find the most cost beneficial solutions that meet the corporations requirements.
  • Project manage the full implementation process of new software systems.
Assistant Controller (20%)
  • Assist the finacial manager with any financial tasks.
  • Aid in the development of the corporations annual budget.
  • Perform financial modelling and variance analysis on financial statements.
  • Develop Key Performance Indicators that provide insightful looks into how the businesses are performing.
